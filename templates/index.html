About Us

Brotherhood's easy listening blend of acoustic and indie pop has been influenenced by a number of artists of a similar style. Their music draws comparsions to Del Amitri and the acoustic style of Jose Gonzalez.

The up and coming band consists of Irish brothers Joel Saunderson and Nick Saunderson who currently reside in Glasgow, Scotland.
